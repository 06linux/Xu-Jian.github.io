`配置文件 _data/people.yml`

~~
{% raw %}
dave:
    name: David Smith
    twitter: DavidSilvaSmith

然后可在文章的头信息中指定作者
---
title: sample post
author: dave
---

{% assign author = site.data.people[page.author] %}
<a rel="author"
  href="{{ author.twitter }}"
  title="{{ author.name }}">
    {{ author.name }}
</a>
{% endraw %}
~~~
